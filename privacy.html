Top 8 is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our app. By using Top 8, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

When you use Top 8, we may collect certain personal information from you, including:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number (if you choose to provide it)
  • Information from your device's contact book, including the names and phone numbers of your contacts

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect to:

  • Analyze how the app is used and track usage statistics

We may also use your personal information for other purposes with your consent or as permitted by law.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information with third parties in the following situations:

  • With your consent

We may also share non-personal information (such as usage statistics) with third parties for any purpose.

Your Choices

You may choose not to provide certain personal information to us, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of the Top 8 app.

You may also opt out of receiving promotional emails or messages from Top 8 by following the unsubscribe instructions in those emails or messages.

Security

Top 8 takes reasonable measures to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
